# HALO

**The Autonomous Social Operating System**

HALO is an AI-powered platform that builds real human relationships through agent-mediated connection. Each user gets a persistent autonomous agent that understands them deeply and negotiates connections on their behalf — across dating, friendship, and mentorship.

---

### The Problem

- 50% of people report chronic loneliness ($154B annual cost to society)
- Dating apps are designed to profit from failure to connect
- Building meaningful relationships takes effort most people can't sustain

### The Solution

- **Daemon-per-user** autonomous agents that work 24/7
- **Zero human effort** — your agent handles discovery, matching, and coordination
- **Outcome-aligned** revenue model — we profit when users succeed
- **Multi-domain** — dating, friendship, mentorship, professional networking

### Why HALO

| | Traditional Apps | HALO |
|---|---|---|
| **Incentive** | Engagement (keep swiping) | Outcomes (real connections) |
| **Effort** | High (profiles, swiping, messaging) | Zero (agent handles everything) |
| **Scope** | Single domain | Multi-domain relationships |
| **Intelligence** | Basic algorithms | Autonomous AI agents with deep behavioral understanding |

### Market

- **$15.5B** TAM (dating apps)
- **$46.5B** Expanded loneliness economy
- **4.2x** LTV:CAC ratio
