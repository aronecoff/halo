The Method

Step 1

Ingest Real Data

204,467 iMessages across 5 relationships spanning 2018–2026. Every message timestamped, directional, and analyzed for emotional, conflict, and attachment signals.

Step 2

Train on 60%

For each relationship, the engine only sees the first 60% of messages. It extracts trajectory, conflict patterns, attachment behavior, engagement volatility, and cycle signatures.

Step 3

Predict the Rest

The engine generates specific predictions: will the relationship intensify or decay? Will conflict escalate? Is disruption coming? Each prediction includes a confidence score.

Step 4

Validate Against Reality

We compare every prediction against the actual last 40% of messages. No post-hoc rationalization. The data either confirms or contradicts each prediction.

What This Proves

From nothing but message timestamps, lengths, and keyword patterns — no content analysis, no AI language models, no self-reported data — the engine predicted relationship trajectories with meaningful accuracy across all five relationships.

Engagement volatility was identified with 100% accuracy. Trajectory was predicted correctly 70% of the time. Disruption events were anticipated in 4 of 5 relationships.

This was built in hours with basic signal extraction. Imagine what a production system with NLP, semantic analysis, and multi-source data could do.
