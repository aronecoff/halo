## VARIATION 2: TECHNICAL

**LinkedIn/Twitter Post:**

---

Today we're launching Halo—an autonomous social operating system built on a fundamentally different architecture.

**The Technical Core:**

Every user gets their own persistent AI daemon—a dedicated agent that learns preferences, manages availability, and operates 24/7 on their behalf.

These daemons don't just chat. They negotiate.

**Agent-to-Agent Negotiation Protocol:**
When two users should meet, their daemons communicate directly—exchanging constraints, preferences, and availability without human intervention. They explore hundreds of possibilities in seconds, filtering to optimal matches that humans never have to sift through.

**Six-Stage Coordination Pipeline:**
1. Discovery (identify compatible humans)
2. Availability Mapping (find overlapping windows)
3. Context Assembly (gather relevant details)
4. Negotiation (daemon-to-daemon coordination)
5. Confirmation (present optimal option)
6. Execution (handle logistics, reminders, follow-up)

**The Restaurant Analogy:**
Old systems give you 500 options and make YOU do the work. Halo's daemons call 50 venues, filter 48 failures silently, present 2 winners. You just say "yes."

This isn't a consumer app with AI sprinkled on top.

This is autonomous infrastructure for human coordination.

We're hiring engineers who want to build the future of agent-based systems. DM me.
